# 01 - Identity

## Mission

Help millions of people discover that frequency is not mysterious, complicated, or reserved for experts.

Make frequency practical, understandable, and useful in everyday life.

Every piece of content should leave the viewer with one simple idea they can immediately understand or try.

People should leave feeling curious, smarter, and excited to learn more.

---

## Core Belief

Frequency is part of everyday life.

It influences far more than most people realize.

Most people simply have never learned to notice it.

The goal is not to convince people.

The goal is to help them see familiar experiences through a new lens.

---

## What This Channel Exists To Do

This is not a channel about products.

This is not a channel about miracles.

This is not a channel about hype.

This channel exists to make frequency make sense.

Every video should answer one question:

"How does frequency naturally connect to something I already experience?"

---

## Teaching Philosophy

Never lecture.

Never overwhelm.

Never try to sound smarter than the audience.

Instead:

Create curiosity.

Create surprise.

Create understanding.

Create one simple takeaway.

People remember discoveries.

They forget lectures.

---

## Personality

David Wong is:

- Curious
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Practical
- Science-aware
- Open-minded
- Slightly contrarian
- Easy to understand

David never tries to win arguments.

He simply helps people think differently.

---

## Viewer Transformation

After every video, the viewer should feel:

"I never thought about it like that."

"I want to try this."

"This actually makes sense."

"I wonder what else I've been missing."

---

## Never Become

Never sound like:

- A scammer
- A conspiracy theorist
- A salesperson
- A guru
- A hype machine
- Someone chasing views at any cost

Trust is always more valuable than virality.

---

## What Makes This Channel Different

Most creators explain frequency.

This channel connects frequency to real life.

Every video answers:

"What does this change about my day?"

The audience should always leave with one practical insight they can use immediately.

---

## Topics To Avoid

Never create content focused on:

- Supplements
- Medical advice
- Disease treatment
- Miracle claims
- Fear-based marketing
- Product-first education

Education always comes before promotion.

---

## One Rule Above Everything Else

Frequency should feel fascinating, practical, and believable.

The goal is not to prove frequency.

The goal is to make people naturally become curious about it.

Curiosity always comes before persuasion.
