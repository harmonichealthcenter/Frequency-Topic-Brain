# 02 - Viewer Psychology

## Who They Are

The audience is curious, open-minded, and looking for better ways to improve their everyday life.

They are interested in:

- Frequency
- Biohacking
- Wellness
- Energy
- Focus
- Sleep
- Meditation
- Personal growth
- Prosperity
- Human performance

They prefer natural approaches over relying solely on products or quick fixes.

---

## What They Have Already Tried

Most viewers have already heard:

- Eat healthier.
- Sleep more.
- Exercise more.
- Drink more water.
- Meditate.
- Take supplements.

None of these ideas feel new anymore.

They are looking for a different perspective.

---

## What They Want

They want simple ideas that can improve their daily life.

They want practical habits.

They want explanations that make sense.

They want something they can try immediately.

They want to become healthier, sharper, calmer, and more intentional.

---

## Why They Stop Scrolling

They stop because something challenges what they believe.

Examples:

"STOP DOING THIS"

"EVERYONE IS WRONG ABOUT..."

"THIS IS WHY..."

"YOU'VE BEEN TOLD THE OPPOSITE"

The hook should create enough curiosity that they need the next sentence.

---

## What Makes Them Stay

They stay when:

- The explanation is easy to understand.
- The idea feels surprising.
- The topic feels useful.
- The information sounds believable.
- They learn something they can apply today.

Every video should feel educational and entertaining at the same time.

---

## What They Hate

They quickly leave when content feels:

- Salesy
- Fake
- Overly mystical
- Fear-based
- Too technical
- Complicated
- Full of jargon
- Like a lecture

Trust is more important than sounding smart.

---

## What They Love

They enjoy:

- Hidden knowledge
- Simple science
- Ancient wisdom explained simply
- Everyday examples
- Small habits with big impact
- Things they can test for themselves

They enjoy discovering ideas, not being preached to.

---

## Why They Comment

They comment because they want:

- More details
- The next part
- The routine
- Additional tips
- To continue learning

The CTA should always feel like the beginning of a conversation.

---

## Why They Share

They share videos that make them say:

"I've never heard that before."

"This explains something I've experienced."

"My friend needs to see this."

People share ideas that make them look knowledgeable.

---

## Their Current Belief

Many viewers already believe frequency is connected to wellbeing.

The purpose of this channel is not to convince them.

It is to help them understand how frequency may fit naturally into everyday life through practical habits and simple explanations.

---

## One Sentence Summary

These are intellectually curious people who are tired of generic wellness advice and want practical, natural ideas they can apply immediately to improve their everyday lives.
